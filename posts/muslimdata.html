Who We Serve & Why
We serve a community with complex, changing needs. That demands growth in how we show up. We can't keep doing the same things and expect different outcomes. Food hampers — bigger, better, more frequent — won't fix a system that fails our children when they need foster or kinship care aligned with their faith and culture. Our work must move from band-aids to building: prevention, mediation, stabilization, and the infrastructure families require to be safe, housed, and whole.
