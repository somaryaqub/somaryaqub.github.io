04 ‚Äî Hardware Architecture

Every component
considered

  • 01
    Magnetic Safety Lid

    Machined aluminium lid with neodymium magnets. Hall-effect sensor beneath tray immediately cuts power on closure. Internal silicone gasket prevents aromatic material spill. Acts as both safety cover and carrying cap.

  • 02
    PTC Ceramic Heating Element

    Self-limiting PTC ceramic resists runaway overheating by nature of its physics. Responds to setpoint within ¬±3¬∞C. Removable mica tray sits directly on element ‚Äî dishwasher-safe, easy to swap between aromatics.

  • 03
    Rotary Encoder + OLED Ring

    Haptic-click rotary dial for temperature and timer. Surrounding OLED annular display shows current temp, target, and battery. No touchscreen ‚Äî intentionally tactile and glanceable.

  • 04
    ESP32-C6 MCU

    Dual-core MCU with native Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.3 LE. Runs Matter stack (Thread + BLE commissioning). PID temperature control loop runs at 10Hz. OTA firmware updates via app.

  • 05
    Battery Pack & BMS

    4S2P 18650 Li-Ion (‚âà28Wh). Integrated BMS with over-temperature, over-charge, and short-circuit protection. USB-C PD 3.0 at 20W input. Pass-through mode: runs device while charging. LED fuel gauge on barrel.

  • 06
    Cup Holder Base + Grip Ring

    72mm diameter ABS base with removable silicone grip ring (fits 60‚Äì76mm holders). Anti-tip flat-foot mode when ring is removed. Thermal isolation layer prevents heat transfer to holder/surface.
