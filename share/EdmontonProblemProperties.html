Key Observations & Caveats

50 units achievable from Tier 1 aloneEven conservatively, the 13 Tier 1 sites yield ~82 units. The 4 RSM h12 sites carry disproportionate weight at 14–20+ units each.
Paired opportunity — 156 St NW, Sherwood9421 (RS) and 9429 (RSM h12) are immediately adjacent, totalling ~1,223 m². Combined under RSM rules could yield 20+ units.
Tier 3 standout — 11634 101 St NW, Spruce AveAt 2,090 m² assessed at only $593,500, this RM h16 lot offers ~64 units at unusually low land cost.
RS 8-unit cap under political reviewEdmonton Council debated reducing mid-block RS unit caps in June 2025. Confirm current bylaw status before any development permit application.
Lot dimensions still neededAnalysis uses lot area only. Confirm width, depth, and corner vs. mid-block status via yourtruhome.com/edmonton-land-size before acquisition.
Unit estimates are ballpark onlyAll counts assume ~75 m² avg unit and do not account for parking, amenity area, alley constraints, or site-specific conditions. Development consultant review required.
